Act One Bar & Restaurant

Located at Brooklyn's Waterfront District, Act One is a Thai bar and restarant. We have a dedicated karaoke music bar, table service, and friendly staff where you can experience a delicious and affordable ambience.

History

Act one is the secret easy speaking Thai food located in Cobble Hill, Brooklyn, New York. We have a fun theme of decor and combination of Thai musical and American musical with a touch of jazz music. In addition, we have a dedicated karaoke stage and open mic for everyone who loves to sing, play, or talk with their heart's content.

Act One is a place for people who love to express themselves and have fun with family and friends. Plus, you can enjoy our Thai cuisine while having an amazing time.
